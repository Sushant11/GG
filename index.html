About Us

Gaming Garage (GG) Nepal is a gaming station, which started operations in Nepal in 2018. Gaming Garage Nepal is one of the popular gaming station in Nepal. Gaming Garage Nepal strives to facilitate gamers with a common gaming zone where everyone can test their gaming skills and battle for the bragging rights. Gamers can choose freely from the wide range of exclusive games released worldwide. We believe gaming is a passion of an individual, which gives him/her a chance to stay focused and showcase his/her creativity while being outstanding in his/her own right proving oneself a GAMER.
