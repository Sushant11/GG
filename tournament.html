The British College Presents Nepal FIFA 19 Championship

The 3 days event was held on Civil Mall form 1st March to 3rd March 2019. More than 400 players participated in the championship to win the biggest cash prize in FIFA in the history of Nepal. Also, it was the first time where top 16 players were awarded with the cash prize. Nirbhaya Shrestha(KTM) won the Championship by defeating Saugat Gurung(PKR) in the final. Also, Nurbu Thendu Sherpa defeated Piyush Giri Singh to secure third place. Similarly, Bijesh Gurung, Ashmit Thapa, Sanjaya Ratna Shakya and Chesang Hyolmo were the Quarter-Finalists whereas Bishal Rana Magar, Rubin Shrestha, Shrawak Dev Bajracharya, Bikash Maharjan, Suraj Jung Karki, Pasang Sherpa, Subash Gurung and Ashwin Shrestha reached pre-quarterfinals. All the winners were awarded by Chief Guest Member Secretary of National Sports Council Keshab Kumar Bista, Special Guest Vice Presidient of All Nepal Football Association Upendra Man Singh and Principal of The British College Joey Foster Ellis. The event was also graced by some of the renowned artist of Nepal such as The king of stand-up comedy in Nepal Sandip Chhetri, The Intro Band and representing the Nepal Beat-Box Association Nishanta Gauchan and his crew.

FIFA 18 Tournament

The Exciting 3 days tournament was held on the occasion of FIFA World Cup 2018 where 256 participants all around the country showed an amazing participation. The three days event was conducted in the premises of Gaming Garage Nepal where 27 players were welcomed from outside the valley. Ashmit Thapa(KTM) defeated Sagar Pun(PKR) in a nail biting final in tie-breaker after they drew 5-5 in regular 90mins and 7-7 after 120 mins. Aviral Amatya and Aayush Hirachan were other 2 Semi-finalists.

FIFA 18 Open Tournament

The 2 days event was held on Bhrikutimandap Exhibition Hall where more the 5000 gamers and gaming enthusiast witnessed Nepal's first ever E-Sports exhibition. Gaming Garage Nepal was the only official coordinator in the event to look after in the sector of console game "FIFA". More than 400 FIFA gamers were involved in this very first exhibition and show case their FIFA skills among each other.
